CryptoMarket

    Welcome to CryptoMarket

    CryptoMarket is your central hub for clear and concise historical market data for your favorite coins such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, LiteCoin, and more. This project was built with Vanilla JavaScript and integrated with the CoinGecko API to provide comprehensive market data on over 7,000 coins and across 400 different exchanges in real time. Currently, all coins searched have market data displayed for the last 30 days.
